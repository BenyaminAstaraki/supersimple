Our Persian Legacy

At Astaraki Rugs, we honor the Safavid dynasty’s weaving traditions, bringing hand-knotted Persian farsh to Canada. As a Persian proverb says, “A rug woven with love warms the soul”—our founder, Benyamin, curates each piece to embody this spirit, using natural dyes and centuries-old techniques. These rugs are more than decor; they’re heirlooms of culture and craftsmanship.
